David Eliason

A little about me: I first worked as an EMT/Paramedic, then helped develop homes and day programs for persons with developmental disabilities. There, I was introduced to assistive technologies, and began to explore ways that I could make a positive difference in a direct, dynamic way through coding.

I enjoy developing web applications on the front- and back-end, and exploring ways that they intersect with electronics and sensors for real practicality. That original passion for helping others remains my foundation for all that I do.
